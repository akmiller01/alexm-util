Who are the global P20?

The poorest 20 percent of people in the world (the P20) are the poorest 1.4 billion in the world. Although our current understanding of who they are and where they live is based on a limited set of surveys, the chart below can be used to explore everything we know about this diverse group of people. Called a Euler diagram, this chart demonstrates the relative percentages of each demographic you select as well as how these characteristics intersect to paint the full picture of poverty.
